WHAT IS BITS Pilani Enterpreneurship Conclave?

BITS Pilani Entrepreneurship Conclave is an initiative by APOGEE to bring together the finest minds redefining entrepreneurship and product innovation, to build conversation on the new frontiers of startups and provide the next generation founders an opportunity to showcase their budding venture at a national platform to top mentors and investors.

ABOUT

About the Event The Event shall be a 3-phase competition set to conclude on 30th March during APOGEE, the annual academic festival of BITS Pilani- Pilani Campus . With exciting prizes and rewards for the winners, the event is an ideal platform for all the budding entreprenuers to showcase their vision and business acumen. The Elements of the Entrepreneurship Conclave will include –

  • Business Plan Competition
  • Guest Lectures
  • Workshops
  • Speed Dating – Investor Networking


Who Can Apply?
UG or PG student in any recognised college/university can participate.
